About Us

Great Salt Lake Rowing is an adult community rowing association that is based at the Great Salt Lake Marina. The goal of the club is to promote the sport of rowing both at the recreational and competitive levels. We welcome rowers ages 18+ from all walks of life and with a wide range of rowing skills and backgrounds. Our membership enjoy regular club rows and competing in multiple regattas throughout the United States each year. We are active in our community advocating for sustainability of the Great Salt Lake and its diverse ecosystem. We encourage guest rowers to join us and would love to see you out on the lake!
