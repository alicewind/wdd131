Learn to Row - Sculling Lessons

Rowing on the Great Salt Lake is a low-impact activity suitable for all abilities, offering both relaxing and competitive options. Learn To Row lessons start in April for ages 18+, with small classes. We offer both beginning and advanced level classes. After completing lessons, enjoy a free month of membership to join Club Rows. If you choose to join the club at the end of your free month, we offer a one-time reduced club membership rate.
